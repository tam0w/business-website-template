## **Key UI/UX Principles**

* Clear Visual Hierarchy \- Headlines \> Subheads \> Body \> CTAs  
* Trust Signals Above Fold \- Stats, certifications, partner logos visible immediately  
* Value-Focused CTAs \- "Get Free Assessment" not "Contact Us"  
* Results-Based Social Proof \- Specific metrics from real clients with context  
* Mobile-First Structure \- Grid layouts that stack cleanly on mobile  
* Consistent Section Labels \- Uppercase teal labels for orientation
